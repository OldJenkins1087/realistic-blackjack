The rules of blackjack are simple just hit until you are closer to 21 without exceeding. If you double down that means your doubling your bet so that you can get ONE single card. If you split that means your turning a hand where both cards have the same value into 2 different ones. Loans have a 5% interest, for every round unpaid. This games uses a deck of cards so that its not random and completely fair (technicaly you can count cards ;) ) The dealer also has have HARD 17 or more, not soft (so ace 11 dont count). If dealer has blackjack (21 from start), but dealer is showing a face card or ace, then player has the choice: Risk it, and if dealer has blackjack aswell you make no money, OR be safe, and no matter what get even pay. (Blackjack is supposed to give 3:2 payout, but if you play safe you get 1:1)

You may also use 1) to break, 2) to split, 3) to dbl down, and 4) to split. Also when betting you can press enter without clicking on textbox to confirm it
